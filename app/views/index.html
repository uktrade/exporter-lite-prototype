Goods database

Version 1
Tested between 19 November to 4 December 2019

View prototype

What we learned

  • Preference to have part number adjacent to name (exporters make sense of which product is which by one of the name, part number or combination of part number)
  • For many exporters, the CLC will be the same for all their goods owing to the nature of their activities, so it is not that important for sense making
  • On right hand sidebar for individual goods, Good name 1 through to 4, some of the language was not immediately understood, especially "under review" and "be verified"
  • The verified status indicator (green "Verified" alongside product name) understood and valued
  • Good name 4 - Having ability to see which applications had included a specific product positively responded to, although questions raised about what these applications were (that is, current?, decided?) and also how these applications were chronicled (that is, this year?, ever?)
